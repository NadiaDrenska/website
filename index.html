Hello!

I am from Bulgaria, where I graduated from the Sofia High School of Mathematics. I then moved to the United States to attend Brown University, where I earned a dual Bachelor of Science in Mathematics (Honors) and Applied Mathematics (Honors). I completed my PhD at the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences, New York University, under the supervision of Dr. Robert V. Kohn. Following this, I served as a MCFAM Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Minnesota and as a Rufus Isaacs Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Statistics at Johns Hopkins University. Currently, I am an Assistant Professor at Louisiana State University! You can reach me at ndrenska@lsu.edu.
